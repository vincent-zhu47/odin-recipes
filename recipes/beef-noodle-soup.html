Beef Noodle Soup

Description

First, let's talk about what Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup is. It's delicious. Tender beef, a rich and slightly spicy broth, fresh noodles, a little bok choy, and that absolutely necessary fistful of Chinese pickled mustard greens along with fresh scallions and cilantro.

The result is a perfectly orchestrated bowl of spicy, savory, fresh, salty deliciousness. Go on, drool on your keyboard. It happens.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef shank
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2-inch piece ginger
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 3 scallions
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 4 dried chiles
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons spicy bean paste
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup Shaoxing wine
  • 8 cups water

Steps

  1. Boil water and add beef.
  2. Saute ginger, garlic, scallions, amd onions. Add tomato and ried chiles.
  3. Add meat to the pot with tomato paste, spicy bean paste, sugar, soy sauce, and Shaoxing wine.
  4. Pour water into pot. Add spices.
  5. Close lid and cook for 100 minutes.
  6. Release pressure valve and serve over noodles with bok choy.
