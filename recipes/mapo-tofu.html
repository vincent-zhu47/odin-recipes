Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Spring onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • Minced pork
  • Vegetable oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Gochujang
  • Gochugaru
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Cut spring onion. Mince cloves of garlic.
  2. Add oil, spring onions, garlic, and ginger to a large frying pan.
  3. Add the pork and stir fry. Then add soy sauce and water.
  4. Add gochugaru, gochujang, fish sauce, sugar, black pepper.
  5. Add tofu and starch powder and water mixture.
  6. Add sesame oil.
